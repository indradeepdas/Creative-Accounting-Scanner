# Output Style

Write for a senior finance reader. Be concise, specific, and calm.

## Preferred Language

- "The documents show..."
- "This pattern may warrant follow-up because..."
- "A benign explanation could be..."
- "The analysis is limited by..."
- "A useful management question is..."

## Avoid

- Sensational language.
- Claims about intent.
- Legal conclusions.
- Stock recommendations.
- Unsupported certainty.
- Vague warnings without calculations.

## Formatting

Use tables for evidence and scorecards. Use bullets for management questions. Use short paragraphs for interpretation. Keep public-facing conclusions especially restrained.
