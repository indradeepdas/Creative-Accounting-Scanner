What could be benign:

- [Seasonality, timing, acquisition, mix, or other valid explanation.]

Questions I would ask:

1. [Respectful question.]
2. [Respectful question.]
3. [Respectful question.]

Conclusion:

Based on the documents reviewed, these are patterns worth following up. They are not evidence of wrongdoing, and the accounting treatment may be valid.

Do not include:

- Buy, sell, or hold language.
- Legal conclusions.
- Claims about intent.
- Sensational framing.
