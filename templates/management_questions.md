## Revenue And Customers

1. What drove the difference between revenue growth and cash collection?
2. How much revenue growth came from price, volume, acquisitions, and new contracts?
3. What changed in payment terms, customer mix, returns, discounts, or concessions?

## Expenses And Capitalization

1. What costs were capitalized this quarter, and what future benefit supports capitalization?
2. How did amortization periods or useful lives change?
3. What would margin have been if capitalization rates matched the prior period?

## Assets And Liabilities

1. What evidence supports recoverability of the largest asset increases?
2. What changed in inventory aging, receivable aging, or credit risk?
3. Which provisions were released, settled, or remeasured?

## Cash Flow And Working Capital

1. What explains the gap between profit and operating cash flow?
2. How much operating cash flow came from working-capital timing?
3. Which working-capital benefits are expected to reverse?

2. Did adjusted metric definitions change from prior periods?
3. What trend would appear under the prior presentation?
