# Contributing

Contributions are welcome when they improve the skill's usefulness, safety, and transparency.

Useful contributions include:

- Scanner improvements.
- Ratio definitions and calculation examples.
- IFRS and US GAAP differences.
- Jurisdiction-specific reporting notes.
- Public filing examples.
- Extraction improvements.
- Legal-safety tests.
- Output templates.
- Evals for public-company language discipline.

Do not contribute material that:

- Copies copyrighted text, tables, examples, or checklists.
- Identifies or references non-public build inputs.
- Makes defamatory claims.
- Makes unsupported accusations.
- Gives stock recommendations.
- Adds sensational public-company writeups.
- Encourages users to treat red flags as proof.

Contributors should write in original language, separate evidence from interpretation, include benign explanations where appropriate, and keep the skill focused on listed-company disclosure review.
