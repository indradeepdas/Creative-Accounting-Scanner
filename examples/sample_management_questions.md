# Sample Management Questions

Company: ExampleCo
Period: Q2 2026

1. Revenue grew faster than operating cash flow. What timing factors explain the difference?
2. Receivables and contract assets increased faster than revenue. What changed in customer mix, billing terms, or collection timing?
3. What percentage of quarter-end receivables was collected after period-end?
4. Deferred revenue declined while subscription revenue grew. Was this driven by billing timing, renewal mix, or contract duration?
5. Capitalized costs increased as operating expense ratios declined. What future benefits support capitalization?
6. Inventory days increased while gross margin declined. How much inventory is tied to confirmed demand?
7. Which adjusted EBITDA exclusions are expected to recur over the next four quarters?
8. What would operating cash flow have been without unusual working-capital movements?
9. Did any segment definitions or cost allocations change from the prior period?
10. What evidence would management point to as strongest support for the quarter's earnings quality?
